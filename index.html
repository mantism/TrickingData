What is Tricking?

"Martial arts tricking, known simply as tricking, is a training discipline that combines martial arts kicks with flips and twists from gymnastics as well as many dance moves and styles from breakdancing. It aims to achieve an aesthetic display of different combinations of "tricks". - Wikipedia "

World Map

According to the global tricking facebook page there are approximately 23,155 trickers in the world.

Thats approximately .0003% of the world's population. If the world was represented by 10,000 people, only 3 of them would identify as trickers.

*The data reflected in this map is from the 326 responses from the "Tricking Data Survey" given in April of 2016.

Gender Gap

With the increase in popularity of gatherings like the semi-annual Euphoria Gathering hopefully one day this chart will represent a 50 : 50 ratio.

When you go to any trick session, attend any gathering, watch any tricking video, you will notice that the trickers are almost always male. Of the 326 trickers who filled out the survey, about 3% of the trickers are female. Unfortunately, this is pretty reflective of the rest of the tricking community. However, the number of female trickers in the community has grown over the years. In fact, groups like Euphoria Tricking and Intrepid Tricking have risen with the goal "to promote female tricking, to inspire, and motivate more females to start. "
