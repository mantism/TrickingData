What is Tricking?

"Martial arts tricking, known simply as tricking, is a training discipline that combines martial arts kicks with flips and twists from gymnastics as well as many dance moves and styles from breakdancing. It aims to achieve an aesthetic display of different combinations of "tricks". - Wikipedia "

Gender Gap

When you go to any trick session, attend any tricking gathering, watch any tricking video, you will notice that the trickers are almost always male. Of the 326 trickers who filled out the survey, about 3% of the trickers are female. Unfortunately, this is pretty reflective of the rest of the tricking community. However, the number of female trickers in the community has grown over the years. In fact, groups like Euphoria Tricking and Intrepid Tricking have risen with the goal to "to promote female tricking, to inspire, and motivate more females to start. "
